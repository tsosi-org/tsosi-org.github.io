Join us for a public webinar introducing Transparency to Sustain Open Science Infrastructure, a new initiative started in September 2024.

TSOSI aims to strengthen the sustainability of open science infrastructure by spotlighting the organizations that fund them. For its launching year, TSOSI have worked with DOAJ, DOAB, SciPost, PeerCommunityIn, Couperin and OPERAS.
